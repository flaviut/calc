Voltage Divider Calculator

This calculator provides assistance for designing a voltage divider, either with a common E-series of preferred numbers or with the set of resistors you already have in your parts bin.

This calculator accepts arbitrary units and expressions. For example, 5e10 / (1 hour) will be interpreted as "27.77 MHz".
